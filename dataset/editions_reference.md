## Notes

- Edition numbers in dataset: `year - 1985` (e.g. 2026 = edition 40)
- 2021 held in October rather than April — extreme heat conditions contributed to 44.6% DNF rate
- Standard race distance: ~250km over 6 stages across 7 days
- Stage 4 is the "long stage" (~91km in 2026) — the dominant DNF attrition point
